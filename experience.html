Salesforce Developer / CRM Systems Engineer

Smart Park Inc.

Jun 2021 – Oct 2024
Philadelphia, PA, USA • Remote

Smart Park runs off-airport parking and shuttle operations. The business lives on reservation volume, payment status, lot capacity, and same-day customer service — so CRM had to feel operational, not just sales-oriented.

I built and evolved the Salesforce CRM that connected reservations, customers, payments, shuttle assignments, and lot capacity into one staff-facing system. Day to day that meant custom objects and Lightning pages for active bookings, Apex and Flow automation for status updates and notifications, Service Cloud-style cases for delays, refunds, and complaints, and REST/JSON integrations with payment, SMS, email, booking, and mapping systems. I also owned the reporting layer operations relied on — reservation volume, response time, payment status, shuttle activity, and recurring issue patterns — and supported cleanup, UAT, deployments, and post-release checks with ops and vendors.

What makes this role different from a resume line

  • I designed CRM for real-time operations: bookings, shuttles, and service desks had to move together.
  • Integrations were production-critical — failed payment or messaging calls needed logging and investigation paths.
  • I worked directly with parking ops and customer service, so automation had to match how staff actually worked.
